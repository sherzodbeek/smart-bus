Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services

To purchase a ticket, create a free SmartBus account or log in to your existing one. Navigate to the Routes page, select your desired route, choose your departure time, and proceed to checkout. You can pay using a credit/debit card or your SmartBus wallet balance. Your digital ticket with a QR code will be sent to your email and available in your dashboard.
Yes, tickets can be cancelled up to 2 hours before the scheduled departure for a full refund. Cancellations made within 2 hours of departure are eligible for a 50% refund or a free reschedule to another available time on the same route. To cancel, go to My Tickets in your dashboard and select the ticket you wish to cancel.
If a bus is delayed by more than 15 minutes, you will receive a notification via email and SMS (if enabled). For delays over 30 minutes, you may choose to reschedule at no extra cost. If a service is cancelled entirely, you will receive a full automatic refund to your original payment method within 3-5 business days.
Yes, SmartBus offers discounted weekly and monthly passes for regular commuters. Weekly passes provide unlimited rides on a selected route for 7 days, while monthly passes cover 30 days. Student and senior discounts are also available. Visit the Fares page or contact our support team to learn more about pass options and pricing.
If you left something on a bus, contact us as soon as possible through the contact form above (select "Lost & Found" as the subject), call our hotline at 1-800-SMARTBUS, or visit the Lost & Found desk at Central Terminal. Please provide the route number, approximate date and time of travel, and a description of the item. We retain lost items for up to 30 days.
